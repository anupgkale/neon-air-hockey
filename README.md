# Neon Air Hockey

A neon-themed air hockey game for browser with two modes: single player vs AI and local 2-player. First to 7 points wins.

## How to Play

Open `index.html` in any modern browser.

### 1 Player (vs CPU)
- **Desktop:** Move your mouse to control the bottom mallet.
- **Mobile:** Drag your finger anywhere on your half of the table.
- AI difficulty ranges from 1 (easiest) to 5 (hardest, uses predictive trajectory).

### 2 Players (Local)
- **Player 1 (bottom):** Arrow keys.
- **Player 2 (top):** WASD keys.
- **Touch (mobile):** Each player drags on their own half of the table.

## Features

- Neon visual style with glow effects
- Synthesized sound effects (wall bounce, mallet hit, goal fanfare)
- Particle explosions on goals
- 5 AI difficulty levels
- Responsive canvas (works on mobile and desktop)
- No dependencies — single self-contained HTML file
